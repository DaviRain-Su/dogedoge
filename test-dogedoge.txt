

## POST 增

curl -H "Content-Type:application/json" -X POST --data '{"id":1, "text":"davirain", "completed":false}' http://localhost:3030/todos

## GET 查

curl -H "Content-Type:application/json" -X GET http://localhost:3030/todos

## PUT 改 

curl -H "Content-Type:application/json" -X PUT --data '{"id":3, "text":"suyinrong", "completed":false}' http://localhost:3030/todos/3

## DELETE 删

curl -v  -H "Content-Type:application/json" -H "authorization:Bearer admin"  -X DELETE  http://localhost:3030/todos/1
